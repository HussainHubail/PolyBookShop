## Core Capabilities

1. **Three-Tier Authentication** (Admin, Librarian, Member)
2. **Email Verification** for member signups with unique login IDs
3. **Automated Loan Confirmations** via email with detailed datetime information
4. **Comprehensive Audit Trails** (SYSTEM_LOG) tracking all operations
5. **Fine Management** with automatic overdue calculations
6. **Hold System** for managing book availability and waitlists
7. **RESTful API** with role-based access control
8. **Real-time Search** with 500ms debounce and multi-field filtering
9. **Rate Limiting** to prevent brute force attacks
10. **Scheduled Jobs** for reminders, warnings, and automated tasks

## Target Users

### Members (Students/Staff)
University students and staff who need to borrow books, manage loans, and access library resources.

### Librarians
Library staff responsible for managing books, processing loans, handling returns, and maintaining library operations.

### Administrators
System administrators with full access to manage users, view analytics, and configure system settings.
